About Team 2424

Get to know the faces and the spirit behind the team.

More Than Just A Team

Team 2424 started with a simple idea: bring together a group of friends who love soccer and enjoy playing the game with a bit of flair. We're all about community, sportsmanship, and creating memorable moments on and off the field. While we aim for a high-end look with our gear, at heart, we're a friendly local team that values fun and camaraderie above all.
